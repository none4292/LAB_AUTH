Welcome to Your Health Center

Established in 18th Feb 2023 One-Health medical intends to provide quality and affordable health care to our patients.Through One-Health, health awareness programme we have reached school children and spread oral health awareness, done check-ups and have run multiple campaigns for the prevention of medical and oral diseases.

OUR CORE VALUES ARE
INTEGRITY, COMPASSION, COMMITMENT, TEAM WORK, PROFESSIONALISM These values are honoured in all we do for our patients, visitors, medical staff, associates, partners and volunteers At One-Health WE deliver care that is accessible, safe, appropriate, coordinated, effective and centred on the needs of each individual within a system that demonstrates continual improvement. We are here to learn and to serve, we have dedicated one special day for people who are needy and can not afford expensive Medical & Dental Treatment we Proudly call it FREE FRIDAYS every week on Fridays such patients are given free treatments .
